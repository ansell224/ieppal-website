About IEP Pal

IEP Pal exists to free teachers from administrative burden so they can spend more time in meaningful, in-person relationships with students. We will empower educators with accessible tools and robust data. We will continually evolve learning support, through speaking directly with teachers, ensuring we strengthen teaching practice and support long-term student growth.

Website Launch

The IEP Pal website is officially live. After months of development and feedback from educators worldwide, we are excited to share our platform with the community.

Understanding the problem we're solving and the opportunity ahead.

What's an IEP?

An Individualized Education Plan (IEP) is a vital document in special education that outlines students' learning needs, services, and progress measures, ensuring personalized support to bridge the gap between their unique requirements and academic goals.

Icon Placeholder
Icon Placeholder

What's the Problem?

Despite their importance, Individual Education Plans (IEPs) face challenges like inconsistent implementation and tracking across schools; as a result, current systems take time away from face-to-face connections between educators and students. IEP Pal is here to centralize and simplify the support process.
