What's the Problem?

IEP Pal was born from a need for teachers to expand support to students with special learning needs. Current systems overload teachers with administrative tasks and data, both of which require time and resources--which take away from their ability to support students. For many teachers this creates frustration over not being able to see the long-term and positive impact of their support to students. In addition, the frustration of spending more time managing paperwork than interacting with students leads to fatigue, lower morale, and disengagement from the mission of inclusion.

How are we addressing the problem?

We interviewed dozens of educators across Asia, Europe, and the US, all of whom have helped shape our software. IEP Pal standardizes the process of managing individual education plans (IEPs) for special education teachers. Our value comes from turning IEPs from static documents to leading indicators with data provided by teachers. We offer full customizability with data collection and record-keeping. This is supported with robust data security. As of now, we're running a pilot at a school in India, with several dozen students, helping educators digitize IEPs and assessment tools. We're continually iterating on our product, and in parallel, demoing new features for validation.

Help Center

Find answers to common questions about IEP Pal, or reach out to our team for support.

Getting Started

To get started with IEP Pal, click "Book a Demo" on our homepage. After your demo session, our team will set up your admin account and walk you through the onboarding process. Your school's educators can then be invited to join through the admin dashboard.
We offer three tiers: Basic for individual educators who need IEP creation and goal tracking, Schools (recommended) which adds data visualizations, the strategy library, and data collection activities, and Custom for organizations needing tailored features. Visit our Pricing page for full details.
Yes! The Schools plan includes a free trial so you can explore data visualizations, the strategy library, and data collection activities before committing. Book a demo and our team will get you set up.

Features & Usage

IEP Pal lets you create IEPs using fully customizable templates that match your school's requirements. You can create new templates from scratch, update documents whenever needed, and house all IEPs in one accessible location. Navigate to the student's profile and select "Create IEP" to get started.
The Strategy Library gives you access to over 150+ proven strategies curated by experts. You can browse the library, add strategies directly to student profiles, and even create and add your own custom strategies. It's available on the Schools and Custom plans.
IEP Pal transforms your collected data into clear, actionable visuals including bar charts, stacked bar charts, boxplots, and more. You can view trends over time and see whether students are on track to meet their goals — all without manual spreadsheet work.

Privacy & Security

Student privacy is our top priority. IEP Pal is built with security-first principles including encrypted data storage, role-based access controls, and compliance with relevant education data privacy standards. See our Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement for full details.
Only authorized users within your school or organization can access your data. Admin accounts control who is invited and what permissions each educator has. IEP Pal staff do not access student data unless required for technical support with your explicit permission.
